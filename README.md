## Key Findings

- **Penetration variance**: PI penetration ranges from 4.6% to 53% across branches
- **Prizren gap**: Kosovo's 2nd largest city (147K people) has only 14% PI penetration
- **Gjakove crisis**: Lost 14% of business clients in one year
- **Business banking**: Only 16.9% of 77,712 businesses are banked
- **Digital divide**: M-Banking adoption varies from <3% to >7% across branches
- **Expansion opportunity**: 9 municipalities with no NLB presence (53K+ combined population)
