Executive Summary: NLB Prishtina's product mix reveals three structural imbalances management must address: (1) Consumer loan penetration averages only 5.7% of active clients — 11 branches are below median, led by Prizren (3.2%), Besiane (3.6%), and Viti (3.5%). (2) Three branches — Gjakove (54.4% TDA), Prizren 2 (56.8%), and Fushe Kosove (50.0%) — are excessively dependent on term deposits, the most expensive PI funding source. (3) POS density averages only 3.3 terminals per 100 businesses. Five branches with zero POS — including 1001 Prishtine (21,396 businesses) — represent the single largest merchant-acquiring gap in the network.

Term Deposit Dependency Warning
Three branches exceed 50% TDA share of PI deposits: Prizren 2 (56.8%), Gjakove (54.4%), Fushe Kosove (50.0%). Term deposits are the most expensive PI funding source. These branches should shift commercial emphasis toward CA/salary account acquisition and savings products. Do not set TDA growth targets for these branches — instead, set low-cost deposit acquisition targets. Exception: if branch-level or bank-level liquidity constraints require TDA, maintain but flag the cost impact.

Methodology — Module 2: Product Mix, Funding & POS

Consumer loan penetration = consumer loans disbursed (2025 flow) / active PI clients. This measures sales intensity into the existing base — not portfolio weight.

Deposit mix is PI-level only (CA, TDA, Savings from Summary sheet). Micro and CIB deposit components are from segment sheets and estimated via avg-per-employee * FTE. Total deposits = PI + Micro + CIB.

POS data is from Branch Productivity.xlsx "POS Business" sheet — 2025 terminal counts by branch. Density = POS / registered businesses * 100.

M-Banking adoption is from "Digital Penetration PI" sheet — M-click users / active PI clients.

Cross-sell intensity = (total loans + new accounts) / active PI clients. This is a proxy — not a true product-per-client count.

  • No pricing/rate data is available in the source files. All funding-cost logic is directional (TDA > SA > CA cost hierarchy) rather than rate-based.
  • Business active/inactive counts are estimated from penetration ratios — treat as directional.
  • POS = 0 for 5 branches: 1001 Prishtine, Prizren 2, Prishtine-Llapit, Bregu i Diellit, Mitrovice 2. These may reflect data gaps or genuine absence of merchant acquiring in those outlets.
