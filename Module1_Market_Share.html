Prishtina City-Level Context
At city level, Prishtina stands at 21.4% PI penetration (48,619 active clients across 227,466 population). This means the city is not underpenetrated overall; the issue is internal branch allocation and outlet role clarity, not market weakness. Four active outlets and one ghost branch (100101, 7 FTE, zero output) share this market. Individual branch pen% in Prishtina, Prizren (10.9%), and Mitrovice (18.5%) should be read as internal allocation indicators, not as standalone market-share measures.

Methodology — Population Penetration in Multi-Branch Cities

Three cities operate with multiple NLB Prishtina outlets sharing the same municipal population base: Prishtina (5 outlets, pop. 227,466), Prizren (2 outlets, pop. 147,246), and Mitrovice (2 outlets, pop. 64,742).

In these cities, individual branch PI penetration is calculated as branch active clients ÷ full city population. This structurally deflates branch-level pen% and makes these branches appear weaker than they are.

  • City-level penetration is the correct measure for cross-market comparison (e.g., “Is Prizren underpenetrated vs Peje?”)
  • Branch-level penetration within multi-branch cities is used only for internal allocation decisions (e.g., “Is 0001 Prishtine carrying its fair share of the city’s client base?”)

City-level PI penetrations: Prishtina 21.4%, Mitrovice 18.5%, Prizren 10.9%. At city level, Prishtina and Mitrovice are at or above target. Prizren remains the most underpenetrated major city.

Structural outlier: Mitrovice Veriore (100703) shows 116% penetration — active clients exceed registered population due to cross-municipality client registration. Excluded from all benchmark charts and scoring.

Business accounts: Active business account counts are estimated (penetration ratio × registered businesses). Inactive business counts are derived from business active ratio. Treat as directional, not exact.
